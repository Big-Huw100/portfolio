About

Ever since I was a child, I've been interested in stories. Over the years, this manifested into a love of film and TV, so much so that after school was over I headed to Lancaster University to study Film, Media and Cultural Studies. After university, I embarked on a few creative projects alongside part-time work: I started a company called Cosmic Anvil, through which I made a comic book series and comic book production workshops for schools, which I also facilitated. I also filmed and edited videos for events, dance competitions and weddings. I started my own YouTube channel with a few of my friends and made a podcast with my partner. The constant search to do something creative has lead me to coding. Who knows where this may take me?
